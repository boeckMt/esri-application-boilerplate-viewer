Navigation with Mouse/Keyboard
  • SHIFT + Drag the mouse to zoom in
  • SHIFT + CTRL + Drag the mouse to zoom out
  • SHIFT + Click to recenter
  • Double Click to Center and Zoom in
  • Use arrow keys to pan
  • Right Click on the map to get coordinates
Editor
  • To start Editing: a editable Layer has to be checked in the Layerlist
  • To create features: click a template then start drawing on the map
  • To move features: click and drag the feature to a new location
  • To edit features: click the feature to display the vertices and the attributes
